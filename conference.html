Highlights

The Annual NASPP Conference and Exhibition brings together respected industry experts to share the latest guidance and strategies that empower you to successfully navigate through our rapidly-changing professional landscape.

Highlights from the 2016 program include:

  • Practical solutions to respond to how current economic conditions and regulatory reforms are reshaping stock and executive compensation
  • Industry hot topics, including updates on the SEC's Dodd-Frank rulemaking projects, preparation for new CEO pay ratio disclosures, and a round-up of the latest regulatory developments
  • Current trends and new opportunities in stock plan design, including the second iteration of ISS's new Equity Plan Scorecard
  • Tax developments straight from the IRS and Treasury
  • 2017 proxy season expectations and best practices for executive compensation disclosures
  • The FASB's amendments to ASC 718 and other critical accounting developments
  • The latest technologies and innovations in administrative practices
  • Key international changes impacting overseas and globally-mobile plan participants
  • Bonus: NASPP Conference and Exhibition registration includes CompensationStandards.com's "Say-on-Pay" Workshop: 13th Annual Executive Compensation Conference at no extra cost.

Take advantage of our valuable Pre-Conference NASPP programs on October 24 as an add-on to your regular Conference registration:

Tackling Your 2017 Compensation Disclosures: Proxy Disclosure Conference

Do not head into the 2017 proxy season unprepared. "Tackling Your 2017 Compensation Disclosures: Proxy Disclosure Conference" is an essential program for anyone who has any role in setting/approving compensation or the preparation or review of proxy statements.

Financial Reporting for Equity Compensation

Understanding the daunting complexities of U.S. accounting principles for equity compensation is critical to the design and oversight of stock compensation programs. Designed for non-accountants, this program will help you become literate in all aspects of stock plan accounting, including the practical considerations and technical aspects of the underlying principles. The program will also highlight the impact of the FASBâ€™s recent amendments on specific requirements under ASC 718, with an emphasis on strategies and processes necessary to comply with the updated standard.
